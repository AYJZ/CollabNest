For Students

Follow these steps to apply to clubs:

  1. Search for clubs in your area of interest.
  2. Apply using the "Join" button on the club page.
  3. Wait for approval and further instructions.

For Clubs

To create a profile and post updates, follow these steps:

  1. Register your club by clicking "Sign Up".
  2. Complete your club profile.
  3. Post updates to showcase achievements.
